# How to Book Airport Parking

## Online Reservation

Booking your airport parking online ensures you have a guaranteed spot and receive our best rates. Visit www.airportparking.com and enter your arrival date, arrival time, departure date, and departure time. Select from our standard covered parking or premium close-to-shuttle parking options. Enter your vehicle information and create an account for faster future bookings. Complete payment with credit card, debit card, or PayPal. You'll receive a confirmation email with a QR code and access instructions.

## Mobile App Booking

Our mobile app offers the most convenient booking experience for frequent travelers. Download the Airport Parking app and create your profile. The app saves your vehicle information and payment methods for one-tap booking. You can receive push notifications about shuttle arrival times and parking extensions. The app displays your QR code for gate access and stores all your past bookings for easy rebooking.

## Phone Reservations

Prefer to book by phone? Call our reservation line at (555) 789-0123 available 24/7. Our staff will assist you with selecting the best parking option for your travel dates, answer questions about shuttle service and facilities, and process your payment securely over the phone. You'll receive confirmation via email and SMS.

## Drive-Up Parking

While we recommend advance reservations for guaranteed availability and best rates, drive-up parking is accepted based on availability. Pull up to the entrance gate and press the button to receive a ticket. Our staff will direct you to available parking areas. Pay at the automated kiosks or exit booth when you return. Note that drive-up rates are higher than pre-booked rates.

## What to Bring

When you arrive, have ready your booking confirmation (email or app QR code), valid driver's license, and the credit card used for booking. If someone else will be picking up your vehicle, provide us their name and ID information in advance.
