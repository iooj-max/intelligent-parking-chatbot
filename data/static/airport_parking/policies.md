# Parking Policies & Rules

## General Policies

All vehicles must be in good working condition and legal to operate. We do not accept vehicles with expired registration or inspection stickers. Maximum stay is 90 days; please contact us for extended stay arrangements beyond this period. The vehicle owner or designated authorized person must present valid ID when retrieving the vehicle. Vehicles may not be accessed during the stay period without advance notice and approval.

## Payment Policies

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Online and app bookings require credit/debit card payment at time of reservation. Daily parking rates apply; partial days are charged as full days. Weekly rates are available for stays of 7+ days with additional savings for 14+ and 30+ day stays. Lost tickets are charged the maximum daily rate multiplied by 30 days unless proof of entry date can be provided.

## Extension & Early Return

If your travel plans change, you can easily extend your parking reservation. Log into your account on our website or app and extend your booking with just a few clicks. Extensions can also be requested by phone. Early returns are accommodated but are not eligible for refunds on pre-paid days. If you exceed your booked return date, overstay charges will be billed to the card on file at our standard daily rate.

## Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations made 48+ hours before your arrival date receive a full refund. Cancellations made 24-48 hours before arrival receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before arrival are non-refundable. No-shows are non-refundable. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

## Vehicle Security & Liability

While we provide comprehensive security measures, we are not responsible for theft, damage, or loss. Do not leave valuables visible in your vehicle. Lock all doors and close all windows. We recommend removing garage door openers and extra keys from your vehicle. Our insurance covers the parking facility but does not cover individual vehicles; we recommend maintaining comprehensive auto insurance coverage.

## Prohibited Items & Activities

The following are strictly prohibited: storing vehicles for purposes other than airport travel, using the facility as vehicle storage, leaving vehicles in non-running condition, parking boats, RVs, or commercial vehicles without prior approval, accessing your vehicle during your trip without staff escort, and any repairs or maintenance work on vehicles in the facility.
