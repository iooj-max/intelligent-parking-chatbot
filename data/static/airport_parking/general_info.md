# Airport Long-Term Parking

Airport Long-Term Parking provides secure, affordable parking for travelers flying out of the city. Located just 5 minutes from the main terminal with complimentary shuttle service, we're the smart choice for airport parking.

## Overview

Our facility features 500 covered parking spaces designed specifically for extended stays. Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, we offer competitive daily and weekly rates with the security and convenience you need when you're away.

## Shuttle Service

We operate complimentary 24/7 shuttle service to and from all airport terminals. Shuttles depart every 10-15 minutes during peak hours and every 20-30 minutes during off-peak times. The shuttle ride takes approximately 5 minutes to reach the terminal. Our shuttles are equipped with luggage racks and are fully accessible for passengers with disabilities.
