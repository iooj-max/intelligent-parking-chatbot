# Frequently Asked Questions

## How far is the parking facility from the airport terminals?

Our facility is located just 1.2 miles from the terminal complex. The complimentary shuttle takes approximately 5-8 minutes depending on which terminal you need. Shuttles run 24/7 every 10-15 minutes during peak times and every 20-30 minutes during off-peak hours.

## Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are handled through the parking chatbot. Creating a reservation in advance helps secure your spot during busy travel periods and keeps your arrival details ready for check-in.

## What if my flight is delayed or I return early?

No problem. If you return early, proceed to the exit and applicable pricing will be recalculated by policy. If your flight is delayed or you need to extend your stay, request an extension through the parking chatbot. Extensions are charged at the daily rate and billed according to your reservation terms.

## Is the parking area covered or outdoor?

All 500 spaces in our facility are fully covered and enclosed. Your vehicle is protected from weather elements including rain, snow, and excessive sun exposure. The covered structure also provides additional security compared to outdoor lots.

## How do I access the shuttle service?

After parking your vehicle, proceed to any of the clearly marked shuttle pickup points located throughout the facility. Shelters with benches are provided at each pickup point. The shuttle arrives every 10-30 minutes depending on time of day. Simply wave to the driver and they will stop. No reservation needed for shuttle service - it's complimentary with your parking.

## Can someone else pick up my vehicle?

Yes, but you must notify us in advance and provide the authorized person's name and driver's license number. The authorized person must present valid photo ID matching the information you provided. They will also need your booking confirmation number or the credit card used for booking.

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards with Visa/MasterCard logos, and cash. Reservation-related charges are processed according to reservation policy. For security purposes, we may keep a card on file for potential overstay charges.

## What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48+ hours before arrival receive a full refund. Cancellations 24-48 hours before arrival receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours before arrival are non-refundable. Cancellation requests are submitted through the parking chatbot.
