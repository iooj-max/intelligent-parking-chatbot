# Parking Features & Amenities

## Security Features

Your vehicle's safety is paramount during your travels. Our facility is completely covered and enclosed with controlled access points. 24/7 video surveillance monitors all parking areas and entry/exit points. Professional security staff patrols the facility continuously. Automated license plate recognition tracks every vehicle. We maintain a double fence perimeter with electronic gate access using your booking confirmation code.

## Travel Convenience Features

We understand the stress of travel and aim to make parking the easiest part of your journey. Our complimentary shuttle service runs 24/7 with frequent departures to all terminals. Luggage assistance is available from our staff. You can extend your parking remotely through our website or mobile app if your travel plans change. We offer car care services including battery charging and tire pressure checks while you're away (for an additional fee).

## Long-Term Stay Benefits

For extended trips, we provide special amenities to protect your vehicle. Our covered parking protects against weather damage. We offer vehicle wash services upon return so you come back to a clean car. Battery charging service is available if you're gone for more than two weeks. We can arrange for scheduled vehicle starts during very long-term stays to maintain battery health.

## Additional Services

Beyond parking, we offer services to enhance your travel experience. Secure luggage storage is available if you arrive before your flight or return before you can access your accommodation. We have partnerships with car rental agencies for one-way trips. Our customer lounge offers restrooms, vending machines, and free WiFi while you wait for the shuttle.
