# Location & Access

## Address

Airport Long-Term Parking
4500 Airport Boulevard
Terminal Area, City, 12350

**GPS Coordinates**: 40.6413° N, 73.7781° W

## Directions

### From Downtown via Airport Expressway
Take the Airport Expressway heading east toward the airport. Take Exit 12 marked "Long-Term Parking". Turn right at the end of the ramp onto Airport Boulevard. Our facility entrance is 0.3 miles ahead on the left side, clearly marked with blue and white signage.

### From North via Highway 95
Take Highway 95 South and follow signs for the airport. Merge onto Airport Expressway East. Take Exit 12 for Long-Term Parking. Our facility is immediately visible after exiting - turn right onto Airport Boulevard and the entrance is on your left.

### From South via Interstate 87
Take I-87 North toward the city. Take the exit for Airport Expressway East. Follow Airport Expressway for 8 miles and take Exit 12 marked "Long-Term Parking". Turn right onto Airport Boulevard and our entrance is 0.3 miles on the left.

## Distance to Terminals

Our facility is strategically located just 1.2 miles from the main terminal complex. The complimentary shuttle service takes approximately 5 minutes to reach Terminal A, 6 minutes to Terminal B, and 8 minutes to Terminal C. Shuttles make stops at all terminals in sequence.

## Airport Information

The parking facility serves all airlines operating from the city airport. The airport offers both domestic and international flights. Terminals A and B handle domestic flights while Terminal C is dedicated to international departures and arrivals. Ground transportation options including taxis, ride-sharing, and rental cars are available at all terminals.
