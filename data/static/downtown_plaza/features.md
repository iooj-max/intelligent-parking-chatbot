# Parking Features & Amenities

## Security Features

Downtown Plaza Parking prioritizes your safety with comprehensive security measures. Our facility is equipped with 24/7 CCTV surveillance covering all parking levels and entry/exit points. Automated license plate recognition tracks all vehicles entering and exiting the facility. Security personnel patrol the premises around the clock, and emergency call boxes are strategically located on each level for immediate assistance.

## Convenience Features

We offer a range of convenient services to enhance your parking experience. Monthly parking passes are available for regular commuters at discounted rates. The parking chatbot supports reservation requests, status updates, and reservation confirmations. Valet service is available on weekdays from 7am to 7pm for added convenience. We also offer on-site car wash services during business hours.

## Accessibility

The facility is fully accessible with 20 designated handicap-accessible parking spaces located near elevator access points. Wheelchair-friendly ramps and elevators serve all levels. All payment kiosks feature accessible height and audio assistance. We maintain designated pick-up and drop-off zones for easy passenger access.

## EV Charging Infrastructure

Downtown Plaza Parking supports electric vehicle owners with 15 Level 2 charging stations and 2 DC fast charging stations. Charging is available on a pay-per-use basis or included with premium monthly parking passes. Our charging stations are compatible with all major EV brands and feature standard J1772 connectors.
