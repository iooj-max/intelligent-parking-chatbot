# Downtown Plaza Parking

Downtown Plaza Parking is a modern, secure parking facility located in the heart of the business district. We provide convenient parking solutions for both daily commuters and visitors to the downtown area.

## Overview

Our facility offers 250 parking spaces across multiple levels with state-of-the-art security and amenities. We are open 24/7, every day of the year, providing reliable parking whenever you need it.

## Facility Highlights

The parking facility features 24/7 CCTV surveillance, security personnel on-site, and emergency call boxes on each level. We offer both standard parking and reserved spaces for regular customers. Electric vehicle charging stations are available throughout the facility.
