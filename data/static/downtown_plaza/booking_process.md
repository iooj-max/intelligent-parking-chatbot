# How to Book Parking

## Online Booking

Reserving your parking spot online is quick and easy. Visit our website at www.downtownplaza.com/book and select your arrival and departure date and time. Choose from standard parking, covered parking, or premium valet service options. Enter your vehicle details including license plate number and vehicle type. Complete your secure payment online using any major credit card or digital wallet. You will receive a confirmation email and SMS with your booking details and QR code for easy entry.

## Mobile App Booking

Download the Downtown Plaza Parking app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store for the most convenient booking experience. Create an account or sign in with your existing credentials. The app allows you to view real-time availability, select your preferred parking level, save your vehicle information for faster future bookings, and receive push notifications about your reservation. You can display your QR code directly in the app at the entrance gate for contactless entry.

## Walk-in Parking

No advance reservation is required for walk-in parking. Simply drive up to our entrance and take a ticket from the automated dispenser. Park in any available standard space marked with green indicators. When ready to leave, pay at one of our automated kiosks located on each level or at the exit attendant booth. We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay.

## Monthly Pass Application

For regular parkers, our monthly pass program offers significant savings and guaranteed parking. Contact our office at (555) 123-4567 or email monthlypass@downtownplaza.com to inquire about availability and current monthly rates. Monthly passes include benefits such as 24/7 access, reserved parking spots, and complimentary EV charging.
