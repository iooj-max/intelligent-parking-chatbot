# Parking Policies & Rules

## General Policies

All vehicles must comply with our facility requirements for safe parking. The maximum vehicle height is 6 feet 8 inches (2 meters) due to clearance restrictions. Maximum stay duration is 30 consecutive days. Overnight parking is not permitted for RVs, commercial trucks, or vehicles over 20 feet in length. All vehicles must display a valid parking ticket or monthly pass at all times while parked in the facility.

## Payment Policies

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience including cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay). Lost parking tickets incur a flat fee of $25. We provide a 15-minute grace period after your reservation expiration before additional charges apply. Prepaid online reservations are non-refundable but can be rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance without penalty.

## Security & Liability

While we maintain comprehensive security measures, Downtown Plaza Parking is not responsible for theft, damage, or loss to vehicles or their contents. We strongly recommend that you lock your vehicle and do not leave valuable items visible inside. Report any suspicious activity to our security personnel immediately using the emergency call boxes located throughout the facility. Security camera footage is retained for 30 days and can be provided to law enforcement upon official request.

## Cancellation Policy

We offer flexible cancellation terms for prepaid reservations. Cancellations made more than 24 hours before your reserved start time receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of your reservation receive a 50% refund. No-shows without cancellation are non-refundable. You can cancel or modify your reservation through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service line.
