# Frequently Asked Questions

## What are your operating hours?

Downtown Plaza Parking is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Our facility never closes, providing reliable parking whenever you need it. However, please note that valet service is only available on weekdays from 7am to 7pm.

## Do you offer monthly parking passes?

Yes, we offer monthly parking passes at significantly discounted rates compared to daily parking. Monthly pass holders receive guaranteed reserved parking spaces, 24/7 access, and complimentary electric vehicle charging. Contact us at (555) 123-4567 or monthlypass@downtownplaza.com for current rates and availability.

## Is there a vehicle height restriction?

Yes, the maximum vehicle height allowed in our facility is 6 feet 8 inches (2 meters) due to structural clearance limitations. Vehicles exceeding this height cannot be accommodated. Please measure your vehicle including any roof racks or cargo carriers before entering the facility.

## Do you have electric vehicle charging?

Absolutely! We offer 15 Level 2 charging stations and 2 DC fast charging stations throughout the facility. Charging is available on a pay-per-use basis at standard rates, or complimentary with premium monthly parking passes. All stations use standard J1772 connectors compatible with major EV brands.

## How secure is the parking facility?

Security is our top priority. The facility features 24/7 CCTV surveillance covering all areas, automated license plate recognition at entry and exit points, roving security personnel, emergency call boxes on every level, and well-lit parking areas throughout the facility.

## Can I reserve a specific parking spot?

Yes, we offer reserved parking for an additional fee. Reservation requests are handled through the parking chatbot, including preferred parking level and general area preferences. Monthly pass holders can request specific assigned spaces based on availability.

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), and mobile payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay. Reservation-related charges follow the confirmed reservation terms.

## Is handicap accessible parking available?

Yes, we have 20 designated handicap-accessible parking spaces conveniently located near elevator access points on the ground level. These spaces comply with ADA requirements and are clearly marked. The facility features wheelchair-accessible ramps, elevators, and payment kiosks.
