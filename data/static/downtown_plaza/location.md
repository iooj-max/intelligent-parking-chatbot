# Location & Access

## Address

Downtown Plaza Parking
123 Main Street
Downtown, City, 12345

**GPS Coordinates**: 40.7580° N, 73.9855° W

## Directions

### From North via Highway 101
Take Highway 101 South and exit at Main Street. Turn right onto Main Street and proceed for 2 blocks. The parking entrance is on the left side, clearly marked with our illuminated sign.

### From South via Highway 101
Take Highway 101 North and exit at Broadway Avenue. Turn left onto Main Street at the traffic light. The parking entrance is on the right side after approximately 3 blocks.

### From East via Interstate 90
Take I-90 West to the Downtown exit. Merge onto Main Street heading south. Our parking facility entrance is approximately 5 blocks on the left.

## Public Transportation

Downtown Plaza Parking is easily accessible via public transit. Bus routes 12, 45, and 67 stop at Main & 1st Street, which is just 1 block from our entrance. The Downtown Plaza Metro station on the Red and Blue lines is located 2 blocks away at Main & 3rd Street, providing excellent connectivity throughout the city.

## Nearby Landmarks

Our central location puts you within walking distance of major downtown destinations. City Hall is 0.3 miles away, the Convention Center is 0.5 miles, and the Financial District is just 0.2 miles. The downtown Shopping Mall is located 0.4 miles from our facility. Numerous restaurants, cafes, and business offices surround the parking facility.
